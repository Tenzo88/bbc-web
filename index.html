Computer Education Institute

Understanding Computer Education Institutions Computer Education Institutions are out-of-school education under the Directorate of Public Education. One of the tasks and functions of the Directorate of Out-of-school Education is to carry out coaching and development of courses, including computer education institutions. Efforts to foster and develop courses are also oriented towards realizing course institutions as a vehicle for continuing education, which has excellent ability to provide educational services for the community at various levels with the aim of increasing knowledge, skills and attitudes that can be used to develop themselves, work for a living and or continue to a higher level.
